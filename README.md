## üí° Strategic Recommendations

1. **Focus on Germany Market**
   - Highest revenue market (‚Ç¨1.6M)
   - Strong margin performance (28.02%)
   - Opportunity to expand from 22 to 30+ locations

2. **Optimize Product Mix**
   - **Increase** Prescription and Wellness (highest margins: 33%+)
   - **Maintain** OTC leadership position (largest volume)
   - **Review** Medical Devices category (lowest margin: 24.96%)

3. **Reevaluate Promotional Strategy**
   - Current promotions reduce margin by 9+ percentage points
   - Focus promotions on customer acquisition, not margin products
   - Consider targeted promotions for seasonal slow periods

4. **Urban Location Expansion**
   - Urban pharmacies deliver 48% of revenue with better margins
   - Prioritize urban expansion in Germany and France
   - Consider rural consolidation opportunities

5. **Seasonal Inventory Planning**
   - Prepare for September/November demand drops
   - Increase inventory ahead of March/July peaks
   - Category-specific seasonality requires detailed planning

6. **Brand Portfolio Optimization**
   - **Grow**: AntiBioX, BioBalance (volume + margin leaders)
   - **Maintain**: Top 10 brands generating 60% of gross profit
   - **Review**: Bottom performers with <25% margin
